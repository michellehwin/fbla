Why Tonal

At Tonal, we strive to deliver a great flying experience to our customers while also maintaining a comfortable environment for all of our employees. We are open minded about how to operate our airline and greatly respect the ideas and initiatives of our employees.

Travel Perks

Everyone needs time to themselves. At Tonal, we recognize this and provide employees with many benefits to use during their time off. Our crew members enjoy free standby travel on Tonal Airlines, a perk of working in the aviation industry. This great merit enables our employees to travel to many vacation destinations.

  • First Officer
  • Ground Operations

  • System Operations
